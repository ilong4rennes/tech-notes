# Week 9 — Geology, Biology, and Darwinism

## Sedgwick

Sedgwick argued that Earth's geological conditions changed dramatically over time.

Organisms had to adapt to these changes to survive.

---

## Lyell

### Uniformitarianism

The same geological processes operating today shaped the Earth in the past.

Changes occur gradually over long periods.

### Philosophical Significance

Lyell introduced the concept of **deep time**.

This made evolutionary change plausible.

The Earth's history is not a short sequence of catastrophes but a long process of gradual transformation.
