# Week 5 — Galilean Mechanics and Newton I

## Galilean Mechanics

### Key Insight

Motion does not require a continuous force.

Objects continue moving unless acted upon by external forces.

This is the concept of **inertia**.

### Philosophical Significance

This idea overturned Aristotle's physics.

It replaced qualitative explanations with **mathematical laws of motion**.

Nature becomes predictable and law-governed.
